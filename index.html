Dr. Siri Moen Gjersøe

Linguist • Tone & Prosody Research • NLP & Language Technology

I hold a PhD in Linguistics from Leipzig University and specialize in suprasegmental morphology, tone, and West Nilotic languages, with a focus on Nuer. Alongside academic research, I work with NLP, NLU, and AI-based language systems in industry.

My work integrates fieldwork, experimental phonetics, and computational methods to study how tone and morphology interact in natural language.
